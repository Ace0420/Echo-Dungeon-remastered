## A Voice-Controlled RPG Adventure for Everyone

**Echo Dungeon V10** is a fully voice-controlled dungeon crawler designed from the ground up for accessibility. Navigate dungeons, fight monsters, collect loot, and level up‚Äîall through voice commands and audio feedback.

## üåü WHY ECHO DUNGEON?

Traditional games often exclude blind and visually impaired players. Echo Dungeon changes that.

**No screen needed. Just your voice and your ears.**

Built with love for the blind and visually impaired gaming community, but fun for everyone who wants a unique RPG experience.

## üéÆ HOW TO PLAY

### **COMBAT COMMANDS**
- **"Attack"** - Strike your enemy
- **"Defend"** - Block to reduce incoming damage by 50%
- **"Special"** - Use your class special ability
- **"Cast [spell name]"** - Use learned abilities from books
- **"Use potion"** or **"Use health potion"** or **"Use mana potion"** - Heal or restore mana
- **"Flee"** - Attempt to escape (chance based on enemy)

### **EXPLORATION COMMANDS**
- **"North"** / **"South"** / **"East"** / **"West"** - Move through the dungeon
- **"Look around"** - Hear your current room description
- **"Search"** - Look for hidden items and gold
- **"Open chest"** - Loot treasure chests
- **"Go down stairs"** - Descend to the next dungeon level (after defeating the boss)
- **"Drink fountain"** - Fully heal at magic fountains (one use per fountain)
- **"Meditate"** - Restore 25 mana outside of combat

### **EQUIPMENT COMMANDS**
- **"Wear ring [name]"** - Equip rings (up to 10 rings!)
- **"Remove ring [name]"** - Unequip a ring
- **"Equip amulet [name]"** - Wear an amulet (only 1 at a time)
- **"Equip bracelet [name]"** - Wear bracelets (left and right wrist)
- **"Equip [item name]"** - Equip weapons, armor, shields, helmets, gloves, boots
- **"Read book"** - Learn abilities from ability books

### **JUNK BAG SYSTEM** üéí (NEW!)
The easiest way to manage and sell equipment:
- **"Add to junk [item name]"** - Mark an item for sale
- **"View junk"** - See what's in your junk bag
- **"Remove from junk [item name]"** - Unmark an item
- **"Sell junk"** - Sell everything in your junk bag at once (at merchant)
- **"Clear junk"** - Empty your junk bag without selling

### **MERCHANT COMMANDS**
- **"Merchant"** - Talk to a traveling merchant
- **"What do you have"** - List available items for your class
- **"Buy [item name]"** - Purchase items
- **"Sell junk"** - Sell all items in your junk bag
- **"Leave"** - Exit the merchant

### **INFO COMMANDS**
- **"Status"** - Hear your character stats, equipment, and abilities
- **"Inventory"** - List all items you're carrying
- **"Hint"** - Get contextual advice
- **"Commands"** - Hear available commands
- **"Help"** - Quick help

### **SYSTEM COMMANDS**
- **"Save game"** - Save your progress (get a 4-digit PIN)
- **"Load game"** then **"Code [PIN]"** - Load a saved game

## üó∫Ô∏è DUNGEON LAYOUT

- **12x12 grid** per level
- **You start** at the center (6,6)
- **Boss room** is always at the far southeast corner (11,11)
- **Stairs** are near the boss room - defeat the boss to unlock deeper levels
- **Merchants** appear randomly throughout the dungeon
- **Secret rooms** can be found by searching

### **ROOM TYPES**
- **Enemy Rooms** - Combat encounters
- **Treasure Rooms** - Chests with valuable loot
- **Empty Rooms** - Safe exploration, may have chests
- **Fountain Rooms** - Full heal and mana restore (one-time use)
- **Trap Rooms** - Take damage on first visit
- **Merchant Rooms** - Buy and sell items
- **Crypt Rooms** - Sometimes have loot
- **Boss Rooms** - Powerful enemies with great rewards

## ‚öîÔ∏è COMBAT SYSTEM

### **Damage Calculation**
Your damage = Base Attack + Weapon + Gloves + Bracelets + Amulet + Rings + Level Bonuses + Random(0-10)

### **Defense System**
Enemy damage is reduced by your total Defense from armor, shield, helmet, boots.

### **Status Effects**
- **Frozen** - Enemy can't act for 1 turn
- **Stunned** - Enemy can't act for 1 turn
- **Poisoned** - Take damage over time
- **Death Marked** - Take 50% more damage
- **Time Stopped** - All enemies frozen for 2 turns

### **Special Potions**
- **Elixir of Immortality** - Auto-revive once with 50% health
- **Potion of Giant Strength** - Double damage for 3 battles
- **Elixir of Clarity** - Spells cost 50% less mana for 3 battles

## üí∞ PROGRESSION SYSTEM

### **LEVELING UP**
- Gain XP from defeating enemies
- Each level increases max health (+20) and max mana (+10)
- Fully heals you when you level up
- XP required increases by 20% each level

### **SPECIAL ITEMS** (Permanent Bonuses)
- **Merchant's Lucky Coin** - +50% gold from all sources
- **Crystal of Experience** - +100% XP gain
- **Ring of Regeneration** - Restore 50 HP per turn in combat

### **DUNGEON LEVELS**
- Each dungeon level has tougher enemies
- Enemy stats scale with dungeon level
- Better loot drops at higher levels
- Merchants sell higher-tier items

---

## üõí MERCHANT SYSTEM

### **WHAT MERCHANTS SELL**
- **Potions** - Health and mana potions of all tiers
- **Special Potions** - Elixir of Immortality, Giant Strength, Clarity
- **Equipment** - Weapons, armor, and accessories for your class
- **Special Items** - Permanent power-ups like Lucky Coin
- **High-Level Gear** - Legendary and mythic items (requires higher dungeon levels)

### **SELLING TO MERCHANTS**
Use the **Junk Bag System**:
1. Mark items for sale: **"Add to junk [item name]"**
2. Go to a merchant: **"Merchant"**
3. Sell everything at once: **"Sell junk"**
4. Profit! üí∞

Equipment sells for 60% of value, treasures sell for full value.

## üéØ PRO TIPS

### **COMBAT**
- **Defend** when low on health to reduce damage
- **Flee** from tough enemies early on - not all fights are winnable at low levels
- **Use potions** liberally - you can always buy more
- **Learn abilities** from books to unlock powerful attacks
- **Stack rings** - wear up to 10 rings for massive stat bonuses!

### **EXPLORATION**
- **Search every room** - hidden items and gold are common
- **Visit fountains** when low on resources for a full heal
- **Mark equipment as junk** as you go, then sell in bulk at merchants
- **Save often** - get a PIN and write it down!

### **EQUIPMENT**
- **Bracelets give attack AND mana** - extremely valuable!
- **Rings stack** - two Ring of Vitality = +20 max health!
- **Special items are permanent** - buy them ASAP
- **Check merchant inventory** at each dungeon level for better gear

### **MONEY MAKING**
- **Search every room** for gold and treasures
- **Sell old equipment** using the junk bag
- **Buy Merchant's Lucky Coin** early for 50% more gold
- **Treasures** from treasure chests sell for high value

## üêõ TROUBLESHOOTING

### **Voice not recognized?**
- Check microphone permissions
- Ensure you're on HTTPS or localhost
- Try Chrome or Edge browser
- Speak clearly after tapping the screen

### **Game not speaking?**
- Check audio is not muted
- Verify browser has speech synthesis enabled
- Try refreshing the page

### **Game frozen?**
- Refresh the page
- Load your saved game if you have a PIN
- Check browser console for errors
